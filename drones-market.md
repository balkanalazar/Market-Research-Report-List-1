Fixed-wing drones resemble traditional aircraft and are ideal for long-distance flights, offering greater endurance and speed. Multirotor drones, featuring multiple propellers, are highly maneuverable and suitable for aerial photography or surveillance in smaller areas. Single rotor drones, similar to helicopters, provide enhanced lift and stability, making them effective for specific applications like agriculture or construction where payload capacity is essential.</p></p>
