<p><p>The excavator hydraulic valve market comprises several key types, including main control valves, hand-operated valves, and walking control valves. Main control valves manage hydraulic fluid flow and pressure, enabling precise movements of the excavator's attachments. Hand-operated valves provide manual control for operators, allowing for quick adjustments in hydraulic functions. Walking control valves facilitate movement of tracked excavators, enabling smooth navigation across various terrains. Each type plays a vital role in the efficiency and performance of excavators in construction and excavation applications.</p></p>
